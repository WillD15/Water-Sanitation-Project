Why is clean water important?


The Goal of Sanitation is a huge issue in developing countries like rural africa and india as many people have trouble finding access to clean water. Without clean water people who drink the water can die from basic disease like diahrea. The Global goals are designed to bring awarness to this issue helping people without access to clean water get something healthy ans sustainable to drink every day.







Why is this a big deal?

Although to most people living in first world countries like America, Canada, Europe, and many more, water might seem like just an after thought, many people struggle to drink the recommended amount of water daily, some people dont even drink water sometimes! This issue coupled with poor sanitation leads to basic diseases to kill people simply because they are drinking unhealthy water.
