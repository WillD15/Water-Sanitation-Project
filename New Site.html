Who doesn't have access to clean water?


It is estimated that approximatley 29% of people dont have access to clean drinking water that is almost 1 in 3 people. Ethopia has the least safe drinking water out of any area on earth.







What solutions can be found?

With over 1/4 of people not getting safe access to water it seems like the planet is not focusing on the issue too much, however the private sector has develpoed many ways of providing people with safer water. My favourite solution is the life straw, a filter that you can drink from directly which according to their website "Removes 99.999999% of bacteria (including E.coli, Salmonella), 99.999% of parasites (including Giardia and Cryptosporidium), 99.999% of microplastics, dirt, sand and cloudiness". For only $20 this is one of the best solutions we have right now to fighting this issue.
